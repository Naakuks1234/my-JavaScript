Top Digital Marketing Tools!

This course provides an introduction to some of the most powerful digital marketing tools available today. Students will learn how to tap into the key advantages of these tools to bolster their digital marketing efforts. These services can be used together to create a synergistic effect and turn an anemic marketing strategy into one that produces better results and higher ROI. We teach how SEMrush can be used to discover key insights from competitors such as their PPC and SEO keywords, estimated traffic from each, ad copy, and more.We show how Ahrefs can drill down into the links your target site has. These links act as credibility boosters. By exploring links to similar offerings, you can figure out a coverage neighborhood that would be most receptive to your own offerings.

Who is the target audience?

People who want an introduction to popular and beneficial tools that can help market their business, blog, nonprofit, or other offerings.

Requirements

No prior knowledge is needed and we welcome anyone with a desire to learn about digital marketing tools.
